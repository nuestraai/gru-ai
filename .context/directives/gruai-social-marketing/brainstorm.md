## Research Findings (from agent web searches)

### X/Twitter (NEW)
- Algorithm penalizes external links 50-90%
- Replies weighted 75x > likes
- Best format: threads with embedded visuals, not link posts
- MCP servers exist for Twitter integration with Claude
- Developer tool accounts need daily engagement for weeks to build reach

### HackerNews
- "Show HN" AI agent frameworks are common — needs strong visual hook to stand out
- Best timing: Tue-Thu 8-10 AM PT
- First comment is crucial — post within 5 minutes with technical details
- 24-48 hour engagement commitment required

### ProductHunt
- AI developer tools category is active
- Algorithm rewards steady engagement throughout launch day
- First hour sets the tone — prepare 10-20 genuine testers
- Product Hunt's effectiveness for dev tools debated in 2026 (some HN discussion says "PH is dying")

### LinkedIn
- Algorithm heavily favors personal accounts over company pages
- 2-3 substantial posts/week > daily volume
- Good for building-in-public narrative
- Lower effort than Reddit/HN but slower growth

### Discord
- Anthropic, OpenAI, Hugging Face, Mistral all have large Discord servers
- Can share in relevant channels but not a primary marketing channel
- Best for community building after initial traction
